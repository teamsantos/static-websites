Our Story

Crafted with passion since 2015

What started as a small roastery in Brooklyn has grown into a beloved neighborhood café. We believe great coffee is more than a drink — it's a ritual, a moment of pause, a connection to the hands that cultivated each bean.

Single Origin

We source our beans directly from small farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala.

Sustainably Sourced

Every bean is ethically traded, supporting farming communities and the environment.

Roasted With Love

Small-batch roasting ensures each cup carries the unique character of its origin.
